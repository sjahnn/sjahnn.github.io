I am an Assistant Professor of Biostatistics in the Department of Population Health Science and Policy at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai. At Mount Sinai, I am also a member of the Institute for Health Care Delivery Science, a member of the Center for Biostatistics, and a member of the Biostatistics and Clinical Informatics Shared Facility at the Tisch Cancer Institute. My research centers on developing statistical methods and bioinformatics tools for differential network analysis, causal mediation analysis, and multi-omics data integration to solve problems in proteogenomics data, microbiome data, and transcriptomics data.
